Who I am

Born and raised in Stuttgart, Germany, I grew up in an English and German bilingual household. After completing my Abitur with concentrations in Biology and Physics, I attented Emory University in Atlanta, where I received a BA in Computer Science.

Here, I discovered my love of art and video games, prompting me to take classes in 2D and 3D Graphics, and publish my own video game - Escaping Hell on Steam. During Covid, I took a gap semester to do an internship with the Stuttgart-based video game studio Studio Seufz, which then hired me as a part time game programmer while I continued my degree.

To my own surprise, my background in design and programming lent itself to the creation of Augmented Reality experiences. I was hired as an intern at Snap Inc. where I worked on Connected Lenses as well as some smaller projects. After the summer, I was offered a fulltime position after my granduation in summer of 2022.
